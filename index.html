Netflix

Disney+

A streaming service offering family-friendly entertainment along with select anime content. Features titles like Star Wars: Visions (anime anthology) and distributes anime through partnerships, including Bleach: Thousand-Year Blood War in some regions.

Amazon Prime

A video streaming platform included with Amazon Prime membership, offering movies, TV series, and a growing anime library. Known for exclusive anime like Vinland Saga, Dororo, and Made in Abyss.

Hulu

A U.S.-based streaming service offering TV shows, movies, and a strong anime selection through partnerships with major distributors. Features popular series like Attack on Titan, My Hero Academia, and Tokyo Revengers.

Youtube

A global video-sharing platform where users can watch, upload, and share videos, including anime clips, fan edits, AMVs, and official episodes from studios or publishers. Some platforms stream full anime legally via YouTube channels.

Spotify

A music streaming service that provides access to millions of songs, including anime soundtracks, opening and ending themes, and anime-related podcasts. Popular for playlists featuring artists like LiSA, Aimer, and RADWIMPS.

A digital video game storefront offering games for PC and Mac, occasionally featuring anime-inspired titles or collaborations, such as Genshin Impact or anime-themed bundles and discounts.

Steam

The largest digital distribution platform for PC gaming, featuring a broad range of anime-inspired games like Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm, Dragon Ball FighterZ, Persona 5 Royal, and visual novels. Also supports anime-themed community mods and content.
